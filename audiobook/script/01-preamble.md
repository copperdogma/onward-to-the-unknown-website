# Preamble

*Onward to the Unknown* was prepared for the 1987 L'Heureux Centennial Reunion by the Centennial Book Committee under the leadership of Marcel L'Heureux.

The book brings together family history, biography, reunion context, and reference material tracing the descendants of Moïse and Sophie L'Heureux. The names and dates were compiled by descendants of the original fifteen family members, and the stories preserve the memories, hardships, humor, and ingenuity that shaped the family across generations.

For this audiobook script, the listening edition focuses on the narrative chapters. Genealogy tables, printed index matter, blank visual pages, and other reference-first material have been left out so the spoken version can move clearly from chapter to chapter.

What follows begins with the family's early history in Canada, then the story of Moïse and Sophie, a personal memoir of life on the farm, one chapter for each family story, and a closing poem from the book's final pages.
